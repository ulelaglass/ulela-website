Tablet HD

Tablet HD is a premium HD screen protector specifically sized and engineered for tablet devices, delivering crystal-clear visibility and 9H scratch resistance across the full display area.

Protecting a tablet's large display requires a protector that maintains perfect optical clarity across a wide area. Tablet HD uses precision-cut large-format glass with uniform adhesive distribution to prevent warping or uneven surface pressure.
