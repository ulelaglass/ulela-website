3D Cured Full Glue

The 3D Cured Full Glue screen protector features UV-cured full-surface adhesive that bonds completely to curved screen edges, eliminating lifting edges and ensuring a seamless fit.

Unlike standard edge-glue protectors, full glue technology bonds the entire glass surface uniformly, preventing the common problem of edge lifting. UV curing ensures a strong, permanent bond with zero rainbow effect. Ideal for curved-screen smartphones.
