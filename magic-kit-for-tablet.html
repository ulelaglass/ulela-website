Magic Kit For Tablet

Magic Kit For Tablet is an installation kit specially engineered for the large format demands of tablet glass protectors, ensuring perfect alignment and bubble-free application on big screens.

Installing a glass protector on a tablet is significantly more challenging than on a phone due to the larger surface area. Magic Kit For Tablet solves this with an oversized alignment frame, a long-blade squeeze card and extra dust-removal strips to handle larger surface coverage.
ContentsLarge-format alignment frame + screen protector + 2× large dust removal strips + long-blade squeeze card + microfibre cloth
Alignment MethodLarge-format snap-fit alignment frame (model-specific)
CompatibilityMajor tablet models (iPad, Samsung Galaxy Tab and others)
ApplicationDust-free, bubble-free large-screen installation
PackagingRetail box — all accessories included
MOQ200 sets
