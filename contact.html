Before You Contact Us

Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from new buyers.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Our standard MOQ is 500pcs per model for stock products. For OEM/custom orders with your branding, MOQ starts from 1,000pcs. Trial orders for new partners are also welcome — contact us to discuss.
How long does sampling take?
For in-stock products, samples can be dispatched within 24–48 hours. For custom OEM samples, lead time is 3–5 business days depending on your specs. Sample shipping cost is covered by the buyer.
What payment terms do you accept?
We accept T/T bank transfer, PayPal and Western Union. Standard terms are 30% deposit + 70% balance before shipment. For long-term partners, flexible terms can be discussed.
Can you do OEM with our logo and packaging?
Yes — full OEM/ODM support is one of our core services. We can add your logo, custom retail packaging, colour options and product labels. Share your requirements and we'll send a quote within 24 hours.
What shipping methods do you use?
We ship via DHL, FedEx, UPS, air freight and sea freight to 50+ countries. For urgent orders, we recommend express shipping. We handle all export documentation and can assist with customs clearance.
Are your products certified?
Yes. Our products and production facility hold CE, RoHS, ISO 9001, BSCI, FCC and SGS certifications. Certification documents are available upon request for qualified buyers placing substantial orders.
