Privacy 30° ESD OG BP

The Privacy 30° ESD OG BP combines a 30-degree privacy angle with ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) protection and an OG (Original Glass) surface finish for a seamless on-device appearance.

ESD protection prevents static build-up that can interfere with touch sensitivity, while the OG surface finish ensures the protector feels identical to the original phone screen. Ideal for high-end device protection requirements.
