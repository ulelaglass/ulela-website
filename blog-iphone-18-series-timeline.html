iPhone 18 Series Timeline: What B2B Buyers Need to Know

L
Lisa · ULELA
June 2026 4 min read

Most of the industry is still focused on iPhone 17 inventory. Smart distributors are already locking in iPhone 18 Pro production slots — and the gap between those two groups is where the early sales window gets won or lost.

Here's what matters for sourcing decisions right now.

The Timeline

  • iPhone 18 Pro and Pro Max are expected in September 2026.
  • The standard iPhone 18 and 18e won't launch until spring 2027.
  • Any supplier claiming "full lineup glass ready now" for the base models is ahead of the actual supply chain — there's no device to mold against yet.
Why this gap matters Distributors who treat "iPhone 18" as one single launch event end up sourcing for the Pro models too late, and sourcing for the standard models too early. Splitting the timeline is what lets you plan inventory correctly for each wave.

Where ULELA Stands Today

ULELA is now taking pre-orders for iPhone 18 Pro and Pro Max tempered glass and lens protectors. Our mold data confidence is high based on current supply chain intelligence, and if final device specs shift before launch, we replace affected stock free of charge.

iPhone 18 and 18e pre-orders are not yet open. We'll announce when that changes — there's simply no reliable mold data to commit to yet, and we'd rather tell you that directly than oversell a timeline we can't back up.

The Sourcing Decision This Creates

Distributors who place orders now win the early sales window — not the ones who start sourcing in September alongside everyone else, when lead times stack up and the early demand spike has already passed.

If you're planning Q3 2026 inventory, this is the moment to lock in Pro/Pro Max production slots before the queue gets long.

* Timeline based on industry rumors and supply chain analysis as of June 2026; subject to change. If final device specs shift, ULELA replaces affected stock free of charge.
