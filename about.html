About ULELA

China-based OEM/ODM tempered glass screen protector manufacturer since 2016. Supplying wholesalers, distributors and brand owners in 50+ countries worldwide.

Who We Are

Professional Screen Protector Manufacturer Since 2016

ULELA is a China-based, factory-direct manufacturer of 9H tempered glass screen protectors, headquartered in Shenzhen/Guangdong — one of the world's leading hubs for consumer electronics accessories.

We specialize in OEM/ODM solutions for wholesalers, distributors and brand owners worldwide. Our product range covers phone screen protectors, tablet glass, camera lens protectors and dust-free installation kits — compatible with iPhone, Samsung, Huawei, Xiaomi, OPPO, Vivo and 300+ models.

With direct access to Guangdong's premium tempered glass supply chain, we deliver consistent quality, competitive wholesale pricing and flexible MOQ from 500pcs — backed by CE, RoHS, ISO, BSCI, FCC and SGS certifications.
